## 📘 What is a Harshad Number?

A **Harshad Number** (also called a Niven Number) is a positive integer that is divisible by the sum of its digits.

### ✅ Example
Number: `18`

Sum of digits:
`1 + 8 = 9`

Check:
`18 % 9 = 0`

✔ Therefore, `18` is a Harshad Number.
